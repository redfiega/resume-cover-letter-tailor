# Personas

## Project: Resume and Cover Letter Tailor

---

## Persona 1: The Academic Professional (Primary User)

**Name:** Dr. Gabrielle (fictional stand-in for the real user)
**Role:** Full-time university instructor and researcher
**Background:** Has a long, exhaustive academic CV covering teaching experience,
publications, presentations, service, and awards. Applying for positions that require
a focused professional resume rather than a full academic CV.

### Goals
- Quickly identify which parts of their CV are most relevant to a specific posting
- Produce a polished, professional resume without spending hours editing
- Write a compelling cover letter that feels personal, not templated
- Have full control over the final document with the ability to request changes

### Workflows

**Workflow A — Full tailoring (resume + cover letter):**
1. Finds a job posting they want to apply for
2. Opens the app and uploads their CV
3. Pastes the job posting
4. Selects both Resume and Cover Letter checkboxes
5. Answers 3-5 clarifying questions
6. Reviews generated drafts
7. Provides feedback on content and formatting
8. Downloads final Word documents

**Workflow B — Quick resume only:**
1. Finds a job posting
2. Uploads CV and pastes posting
3. Selects Resume only
4. Answers questions, reviews, downloads

### Pain Points
- CV is too long and comprehensive to submit as-is
- Hard to know what to cut without losing something important
- Cover letters feel generic when written from scratch
- AI tools generate drafts but offer no way to refine them

## Persona 2: The Student Job Seeker (Secondary User)

**Profile:** Undergraduate or graduate student applying for their first professional
role or internship. Has a shorter resume but struggles to tailor it to specific
postings and write compelling cover letters.

### Goals
- Make their limited experience sound as relevant as possible
- Match the language and tone of the job posting
- Produce a professional document that competes with more experienced candidates

### What Works for This User
- Clear, guided workflow with no ambiguity
- Questions that help them think of relevant experience they might have overlooked
- Encouragement and professional tone in the output

---

## Persona 3: The Career Changer (Secondary User)

**Profile:** Professional transitioning to a new industry or role. Has substantial
experience but it is in a different field. Needs help reframing their experience
in language relevant to the new target role.

### Goals
- Translate experience from one field into language that resonates in another
- Highlight transferable skills that may not be obvious
- Avoid sounding like they are the wrong fit for the role

### What Works for This User
- Clarifying questions that probe for transferable skills
- Cover letter that directly addresses the career transition
- Honest, specific framing rather than vague generalities
