**About Cascadia Community Foundation**

Cascadia Community Foundation is a regional nonprofit dedicated to building
economic equity across the Pacific Northwest. We invest in communities through
direct programming, grant making, and capacity building partnerships. Our
Economic Opportunity Initiative supports workforce development, small business
growth, and financial literacy for underserved populations.

**The Opportunity**

We are seeking a Program Manager to lead our Economic Opportunity Initiative.
This is a high-impact role for someone who combines strong analytical skills
with a passion for community development and economic justice. You will manage
a portfolio of programs, oversee grant-funded projects, and build relationships
with community partners and funders.

**Responsibilities**

- Manage day-to-day operations of the Economic Opportunity Initiative
- Oversee a portfolio of 8-10 grant-funded programs totaling $2M annually
- Track program outcomes and prepare reports for funders and board of directors
- Build and maintain relationships with community partners, government agencies,
  and corporate sponsors
- Analyze program data to assess impact and inform strategic decisions
- Manage program budget and ensure compliance with grant requirements
- Supervise two program coordinators
- Represent the foundation at community events and stakeholder meetings

**Required Qualifications**

- Bachelor's degree in nonprofit management, public administration, social
  work, or related field
- Minimum 3 years of experience in program management or related role
- Experience managing budgets and grant reporting
- Strong data analysis and reporting skills
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Demonstrated commitment to equity and community development

**Preferred Qualifications**

- Experience in economic development, workforce development, or financial

- Familiarity with Salesforce or other CRM platforms
- Experience working with diverse communities
- Bilingual (English/Spanish) a plus
