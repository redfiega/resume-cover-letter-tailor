You will own the design and delivery of learning programs that drive measurable

**What We Are Looking For**

- 4+ years of experience in learning and development, instructional design,

- Strong track record of designing effective training programs
- Data-driven mindset with experience measuring learning outcomes
- Excellent communication and stakeholder management skills
- Experience with e-learning authoring tools (Articulate, Captivate, or similar)
- Ability to work autonomously and manage competing priorities

**Nice to Have**

- Experience in a high-growth tech company
- Familiarity with agile project management
- Background in adult learning theory
